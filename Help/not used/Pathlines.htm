Pathlines

To show or hide pathlines

  • Click the Show menu and select Pathlines. This alternately shows or hides the pathlines.

  • A pathline indicates that path of a fluid particle as it moves through the model domain.
  • A pathline may be displayed as a line or a narrow tube. (How to control pathline display)
  • The pathline is colored by using a red-to-blue spectrum to indicate a range of travel time. Either red or blue may serve as the early time. Travel times outside the range are clamped to the corresponding endpoint color.
  • Thresholding may be applied to show only those pathline segments for which the travel time is within a specified range.
