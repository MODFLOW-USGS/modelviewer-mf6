Particles

This options is available only for MODFLOW 2000-GWT and MOC3D.

To show or hide particles

  • Click the Show menu and select Particles. This alternately shows or hides the particles.
  • Particles are displayed as small cubes. Showing all particles in the grid may result in a very cluttered picture. To avoid this, particles may be limited by cropping and thresholding. The figure below shows particles in the top portion of a grid. (How to control particles display)

  • Each particle is colored according to the solute concentration associated with the particle. The color is determined by mapping the concentration to the color bar.
  • Cropping may be applied to show only those particles in a specified subregion of the grid.
  • Thresholding may be applied to show only those particles having concentrations within a specified range.
