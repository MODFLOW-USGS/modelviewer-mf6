Particles Toolbox

The Particles toolbox controls the display of particles for MODFLOW 2000-GWT or MOC3D simulations.
  • If the Particles toolbox is not visible, display it by clicking the Toolbox menu and selecting Particles.
  • The text boxes in the Crop section allow you show only those particles in a subregion defined by the cropping planes, which are similar to the cropping planes for solid and isosurfaces. You may
    • Specify the Min and Max positions of the x, y, and z cropping planes. (Min = 0, Max = 1)
    • Specify the Delta value, which is used to increase or decrease the Min or Max position when the up or down arrow is clicked. (0.1)
    Important Note: Cropping does not go into effect when the up or down arrow is clicked. You must click Apply.
  • The edit boxes in the Threshold section allow you to show only those particles having concentrations within the threshold range. To apply thresholding:
    • Specify the minimum and maximum values of the threshold range.
    • Click Apply
  • The Full button sets the minimum and maximum values of the threshold range respectively to the minimum and maximum particle concentrations at the current time step.
  • Particle size may be increased or decreased by clicking the up or down arrow next to the label "Size".
